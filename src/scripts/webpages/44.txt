Charlie Hebdo shooting: Remembering the victims | Charlie Hebdo 




















Thursday, January 8, 2015




                            Views:
                          














Paris Deputy Mayor Patrick Klugman told ABC News on Wednesday that two of the assailants went inside the offices of Charlie Hebdo and listed off the names of their targets before shooting them execution-style.
Among those named was Charlie Hebdo editor Stephane Charbonnier, who was killed Wednesday. Investigators said he tried to fire at the gunmen but was shot.
A huge police operation is under way north-east of Paris in the search for the main suspects in Wednesday's deadly attack on Charlie Hebdo magazine.
 Google is part of a French media fund that will make a donation to Charlie Hebdo in the wake of the attacks on the French satirical magazine.
Police Nationale officer Ahmed Merabet, 41, was patrolling on a bicycle near Charlie Hebdo at the time of the attack.
The terror attack on the offices of Charlie Hebdo newspaper were as brutal as they were efficient.
