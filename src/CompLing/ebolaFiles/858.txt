posted by Sylvia Eneghalu#Ebola:Nigerian Customs Destroys Trailer Load Of Smuggled Monkey Meat





In the fight against Ebola,the Nigerian customs intercepted a trailer load of smoked monkey meat being smuggled into the country through the Jibia border in Katsina state.Monkey is suspected to be one of the major sources of Ebola.
According to The Nation, the trailer loaded with the meat was seized by the Federal Operation Unit “zone B” led by OC of the team, Assistant Comptroller, Gambo Azare. The meat was destroyed under the supervision of the Comptroller, Federal Operation, Zone B of the service, ATS Maina and the deputy governor of Katsina State represented by the Commissioner for health.
