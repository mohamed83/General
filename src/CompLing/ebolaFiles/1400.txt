Francis Kasolo: Coordinator, WHO Sub-regional Ebola Outbreak Coordination Centre (Guinea), Jorges Castilla Echenique: Health Sector Coordinator for ECHO in West Africa (Guinea), Johan Von Schreeb: Professor of International Disaster Medicine, Karolinska Institutet in Sweden (Sweden), Anja Wolz: MSF Emergency Coordinator recently-returned from Kailahun, Sierra Leone (Germany), Korto Williams: Country Director for ActionAid Liberia (Liberia)
