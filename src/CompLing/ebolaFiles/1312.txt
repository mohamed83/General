GENEVA, Aug 13 (Reuters) - The World Health Organization  (WHO) on Wednesday reported 128 new Ebola cases and 56 deaths in  West Africa in the two days to August 11, raising the death toll  from the worst ever outbreak of the disease to 1,069.                Since the outbreak was identified in March, there have been  a total of 1,975 confirmed, probable and suspected Ebola cases  in Guinea, Liberia, Sierra Leone and Nigeria, the United Nations  health agency said in a statement.      (Reporting by Stephanie Nebehay; Editing by Daniel Flynn)
